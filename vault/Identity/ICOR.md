# ICOR Life Architecture

This file defines the complete hierarchy of life dimensions, key elements, goals, and projects. It is the source of truth for how journal entries, actions, and concepts are categorized.

## How ICOR Works

- **Dimensions** are the broad categories of your life
- **Key Elements** are specific focus areas within each dimension
- **Goals** are measurable outcomes linked to Key Elements (tracked in Notion Goals DB)
- **Projects** are time-bound efforts that advance Goals (tracked in Notion Projects DB)
- **Habits/Routines** are ongoing processes that maintain standards (Notion Projects with "Ongoing" status)
